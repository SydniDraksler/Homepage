About Me

Hiya! Welcome to my humble abroad! here you will find everything from my lifes work, to mere little tidbits that hold little meaning. In the earlier years of my life, my social skills were, shall we say, extremely underdeveloped. Because of that, I spent of my time locked up in my room painting, drawing, colouring, you name it! Over time, it caused my imagination to overflow, and I was beginning to create my own friends. My only companion at this time was my cat, Molly, who enjoyed lying next to me and watching me work.
